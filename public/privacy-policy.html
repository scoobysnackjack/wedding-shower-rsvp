Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This policy explains how wedding shower RSVP messaging information is collected and used.

Information We Collect

We collect guest name, phone number, RSVP response status, and RSVP response time.

How We Use Information

We use this information only to send wedding shower invitations, RSVP reminders, and track responses for event planning.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

Data Retention

RSVP data is retained only as needed for event coordination and record keeping.

Contact

For privacy questions or data removal requests, contact the event host directly.
